What's Next

Post-MVP roadmap: three capabilities that extend the platform

Phase 1

Supplier Reorder Automation

Configure low-stock thresholds per flower variety. When inventory dips below the limit, a low-stock alert surfaces with a one-click reorder that notifies the supplier and logs the order — closing the gap between spoilage tracking and restocking.

Per-variety reorder rules
Supplier contact management
Reorder history & status log
Phase 2

Customer Order Portal

A publicly accessible bouquet catalog where customers browse and place orders — no account, no payment required. Submitted orders flow directly into the shop's order board, replacing phone and paper with a clean digital channel.

Public bouquet catalog per shop
Order form with confirmation email
Shop-controlled portal toggle
Phase 3

Analytics & Spoilage Reporting

Turns existing operational data into actionable business intelligence. Spoilage trends by variety, order volume over time, delivery on-time rate, and estimated waste cost — all with date-range filtering and CSV export. No new data entry required.

Spoilage rate & waste cost metrics
Order volume & delivery performance
Date-range filter & CSV export

All three phases derive exclusively from data already captured by the MVP — no new infrastructure required to begin.

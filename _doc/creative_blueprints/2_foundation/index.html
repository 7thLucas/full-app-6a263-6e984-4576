The Challenge & The Answer

The Problem

Florist shop owners juggle perishable inventory, hand-written order books, and ad-hoc delivery coordination every single day. Flowers spoil when stock isn't tracked. Orders fall through the cracks when there's no single view. Late deliveries damage hard-earned customer trust.

  • — Flower waste from poor stock visibility drives up costs
  • — Manual order tracking leads to missed or wrong arrangements
  • — No delivery schedule visibility means late arrivals and unhappy customers

The Solution

A focused management app built specifically for florist shop owners. Track live inventory levels, manage bouquet orders end-to-end, and plan delivery schedules — all in one place. Real-time stock alerts cut spoilage. Clear order timelines prevent mistakes. Delivery views keep every arrangement arriving fresh and on time.

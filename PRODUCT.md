# BloomOps (Florista) — Product Overview

## What It Is
BloomOps is an operations platform built for independent and small-chain florist shop owners. It unifies flower inventory management, bouquet order tracking, and delivery scheduling into a single, purpose-built dashboard — with one primary goal: minimize spoilage and ensure every arrangement arrives fresh and on time.

## The Problem
Flowers are highly perishable. Without shelf-life-aware tracking, shop owners over-order, mis-allocate stock, and routinely lose 20–40% of inventory to spoilage. Simultaneously, bouquet orders taken by phone or on paper get lost, confused, or mis-prioritized. Delivery logistics — especially at peak demand (Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, holidays) — become unmanageable without a structured scheduling system. The result: wasted stock, unhappy customers, and avoidable revenue loss.

## The Solution
Florista gives florist shop owners the operational clarity they need to run a lean, reliable shop. The platform is built around three interconnected domains:

1. **Flower Inventory** — Track every variety by stock quantity, purchase date, and shelf life. Get spoilage alerts before flowers go bad. Prioritize FIFO usage so freshest arrangements go out from the freshest stock.
2. **Bouquet Orders** — Capture and manage every bouquet order with full details: customer info, occasion, arrangement specifications, and fulfillment status.
3. **Delivery Scheduling** — Schedule deliveries with time windows, assign drivers, and view daily route summaries to keep deliveries on track.
4. **Spoilage Dashboard** — At-a-glance view of at-risk stock with smart use-first recommendations that route near-expiry stems into priority orders before they're lost.

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Independent florist shop owners managing their own inventory and orders (1–5 staff shops)
- **Secondary**: Small multi-location florist chains with a shop manager per location

## Strategic Principles
- **Freshness-first**: Every feature prioritizes minimizing spoilage and maximizing arrangement freshness at delivery.
- **Simple, not complex**: Built for non-technical florists — clean UI, minimal setup, fast onboarding.
- **Operational, not decorative**: A working tool for daily shop operations, not a marketing or customer-facing platform.
- **Mobile-ready**: Owners and staff access it from tablets and phones in the shop floor.

## Brand
- **Name**: Florista
- **Tagline**: "Freshness. Zero Waste. On Time."
- **Tone**: Professional, calm, practical. No startup jargon. Speaks to florist owners like a trusted business partner.

## Target Roles (RBAC)
- **Shop Owner**: Full access — inventory, orders, deliveries, spoilage dashboard, settings
- **Staff**: Inventory management + order creation/tracking
- **Delivery Driver**: Delivery schedule only — their assigned routes and delivery status updates

## MVP Scope
- Flower inventory tracker with shelf-life awareness and spoilage alerts
- Order board — create, track, and fulfill bouquet orders
- Delivery scheduler — calendar view, driver assignment, route summary
- Spoilage dashboard — at-risk stock overview and use-first recommendations
- Role-based access control — shop owners, staff, and delivery drivers each see only what's relevant

## Out of Scope for MVP
- Point-of-sale integration
- Customer-facing ordering portal
- Demand forecasting / predictive analytics
- Multi-location management console
